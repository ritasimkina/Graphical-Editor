FR2 The canvas must support the following operations regarding graphic elements:
• addition		Bareis
• deletion      Schmon
• editing		Bareis
• movement		(Bareis over editing)

FR3 The canvas must support at least three layers, which hold the graphic elements. The visibility of a layer can be toggled on or off.
Bareis
